Please join us for
The University of Texas at San Antonio
College of Engineering  
SPRING TECH SYMPOSIUM


Tuesday, April 24, 2018

All are welcome!

Come for an hour or stay the whole day.

The Tech Symposium is an open house featuring the latest and greatest in engineering design, technology and entrepreneurship.

The Tech Symposium features hands-on demonstrations of engineering senior design projects and the Center for Innovation, Technology and Entrepreneurship (CITE) $100K Technology Venture Competition.  


Event Highlights:

  • 65 engineering senior design projects completed by students in Biomedical Engineering, Civil and Environmental Engineering, Electrical and Computer Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering  
  • 33 posters featuring early stage Electrical and Computer Engineering and Mechanical Engineering senior design projects  
  • Cash prizes for student senior design projects  
  • Business pitches for CITE's $100K Technology Venture Competition  

SCHEDULE OF EVENTS

  8:00 a.m. 
Welcome Remarks

8:10 a.m. 
Keynote Address by Darren Simmons, '95
Executive Vice President, Aalberts Dispense Technologies

8:45 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. 
Project Demonstrations and Judging

This is your opportunity to talk directly with hundreds of UTSA students and hear about their design process. A final working prototype of projects will be on display in addition to posters of early stage and completed senior design projects.

1:00 p.m. 
CITE $100K Technology Venture Competition
Hear students' business plans as they pitch their innovative projects and vie for the grand prize.

2:15 p.m.  
Tech Symposium Awards Ceremony
Cash prizes totaling $12,000 will be awarded to the top projects in the Departments of Biomedical Engineering, Civil and Environmental Engineering, Electrical and Computer Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Runners Up and Grand Prize awards will be presented to the CITE $100K Technology Venture Competition winners.
