Tropical Storm Nate, expected later today, could hit Gulf Coast as a hurricane

A tropical depression that formed in the southern Caribbean Wednesday could become Tropical Storm Nate later today, on track to hit the Gulf Coast, and possibly Florida, as a hurricane by the weekend.
